The mastermind behind Hemmingway's Fourth Sport, John is an aggressively average endurance runner and has no business starting his own race. He did it anyway. Driven by equal parts passion, drive, and naivite, he's just hoping he doesn't die doing this.

When John brought up this idea, Austin enthusiastically said "Fuck it, fine." He ran a couple marathons and decided to make it his entire personality. Naturally he now needs to call himself an ultra runner but he believes that Western States is "too elitist" (they wouldn't take him anyway).
